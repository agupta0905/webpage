Publications

A comparative study of SVDquartets and other coalescent-based species tree estimation methods [link]
J. Chou, A. Gupta, S. Yaduvanshi, R. Davidson, M. Nute, S. Mirarab and T. Warnow
RECOMB-Comparative Genomics, and BMC Genomics, 2015
  • The study compares summary based species tree estimation methods with concatenation based methods
  • Investigates a the accuracy of a new method, SVDquartets which works on very short gene sequences
  • We compare SVDquartets with summary based methods like ASTRAL-II, NJst and concatenation based maximum likelihood
  • Used a collection of simulated datasets, varying ILS levels, numbers of taxa, and number of sites per locus



Other Research Projects

Query by Sketch [link] [video]
Mentored by Prof. Aditya Paramesawaran at UIUC
  • Working on a visual query engine, which takes user sketches as input rather than traditional SQL
  • The engines takes a visual predicate as input and finds the record which best matches the visual predicate
  • Similarity is measured by various metrics like DTW distance and euclidean distance and is robust to non-linear scaling in axis
  • Example: Find product whose sales trend in the past year matches some user given sketch
